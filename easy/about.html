About Us

The Building & Blueprint Analyzer is a cutting-edge web application designed to streamline the analysis of building images and blueprints. Our goal is to provide architects, engineers, and developers with advanced tools to help them visualize and understand their projects better.

Using deep learning techniques, our system analyzes uploaded files to detect floors in buildings and classify rooms in blueprints. With a user-friendly interface, we aim to make architectural analysis accessible to everyone.

We are committed to innovation and continuously improving our services to meet the needs of our users.
