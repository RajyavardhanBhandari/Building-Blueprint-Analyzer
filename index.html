How To Use

  • Step 1: Select a Tool: Choose the specific tool that matches your requirements and Navigate to the tool's respective page by clicking on the provided link or button.
  • Step 2: Upload Your File: Prepare your file for upload. Ensure it is in one of the supported formats: .jpg, .png, .pdf, or .dwg. Then Use the Upload File button to browse and select the image or blueprint from your device.
  • Step 3: Wait for Processing: Once the file is uploaded, the system will automatically begin analyzing it. The process might take a few seconds to a few minutes, depending on the file's complexity and size.
  • Step 4: Download the Results: Click the Download button to save the report to your device.

By following these steps, you can efficiently analyze your building images and blueprints and gain actionable insights!
